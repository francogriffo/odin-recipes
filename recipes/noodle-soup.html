Noodle Soup

Description:

It's cold and you want to make a hot and fast meal? Then the noodle soup is the best option!

Ingredients:

  • half a cup of noodles for soup
  • Two cups of water
  • 2 vegetable broths

Steps:

  1. Place the water in a pot, cover it and put it on the fire until it boils.
  2. When it breaks the boil, add the broth and the noodles.
  3. Stir from time to time so that the broth binds well to the preparation and so that the noodles are well separated.
  4. Let boil until the noodles are ready (see the time on the package). Once ready, serve.
