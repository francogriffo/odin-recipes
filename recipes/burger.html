Burger

Description:

Let's make some burgers!

It is always a good option to make some delicious hamburgers.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Bun
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Any other ingredients you want to add

Steps:

  1. Put the meat (already in the shape of a hamburger) on the grill or however you want to make them.
  2. Wash and cut the tomatoes and lettuce.
  3. While the burgers are preparing, put the bacon and cheese on top so they cook.
  4. When everything is ready, put everything on the bun and enjoy your burgers!
