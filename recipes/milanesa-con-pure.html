Milanesa con Pure

Description:

Milanesa con Pure is a traditional recipe from Argentina, and one of the favorites!

It is a guaranteed meal if you are looking to do something delicious and simple to do.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Provencal
  • Salt
  • Potatoes

Steps:

  1. Prepare a mixture with water, salt, provencal and eggs, with amounts to taste.
  2. Cut the meat (it can be beef, chicken and pork) into fillets, and put them into the mixture we made in the first step.
  3. Let the fillets take on flavor, and then coat them with breadcrumbs.
  4. Boil the potatoes in water and salt, and then mash them to make the puree.
  5. Put the fillets to fry in a pan with oil.
  6. And enjoy your milanesas con pure!
